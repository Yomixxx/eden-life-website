Eden Kids

A safe, fun, and Bible-rich environment where children from nursery through pre-teen encounter God and grow in faith every Sunday.

Children's Ministry

Every Child Matters

Eden Kids runs concurrently with every adult service on both the Mainland and Island campuses. Our dedicated children's team creates age-appropriate experiences that introduce children to the love of God in ways they can understand, enjoy, and remember.

Every class uses Bible-grounded curriculum, interactive worship, and activities tailored to each age group. No advance registration is required for first-time visitors — just bring your child to the Eden Kids check-in desk when you arrive.

Child Safety

The safety of every child is our highest priority. All Eden Kids volunteers are trained, background-checked, and committed to providing a secure environment. Children are only released to the parent or guardian who checked them in.

For questions about our child safety policy, speak to a member of the Eden Kids team on arrival or contact us at info@edenlifeng.org.
