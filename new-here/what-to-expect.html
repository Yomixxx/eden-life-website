What to Expect

A typical Sunday at Eden Life is warm, Spirit-filled, and Word-centred. Here's a look at how we spend our time together.

The Service

A Sunday at Eden Life

1

Welcome & Praise

We open every service with vibrant, Spirit-led worship. Expect live music, congregational singing, and an atmosphere that invites the presence of God.

2

Bible Teaching

Pastor Gbenga and our teaching team deliver practical, Bible-grounded messages that address real life. Bring a notebook — the teaching is worth writing down.

3

Altar & Ministry Time

After the message, we invite anyone who wants to respond — for salvation, rededication, or prayer — to come forward. Ministry team members are available to pray with you.

4

Community & Connection

After service, stay and connect. Meet people, grab a cup of tea with a team member, or find out about Belong Groups. First-time guests are especially encouraged to chat with our welcome team.

How long is the service? A typical service runs about 90 minutes — from first praise to final prayer. You're welcome to arrive any time, though we recommend coming a few minutes early to settle in.

Good to Know

Everything You Need to Know

Dress

Dress Code

There is no dress code at Eden Life. Come as you are — smart casual is the norm, but what matters is your presence, not your outfit. You'll find the congregation dressed in everything from traditional attire to jeans.

Kids

Children's Ministry

Eden Kids runs during every service for children aged 0–12. Your child will enjoy age-appropriate Bible stories, worship, and activities while you focus on the adult service.

Learn About Eden Kids
Youth

Eden Pulse

Eden Pulse is our youth and young adult community for ages 13–30. They gather on Sundays and mid-week for discipleship, fellowship, and events designed just for them.
