Eden Pulse

The youth and young adult community of Eden Life — a space to be real, grow deep, and live boldly for Christ.

Ages 13 – 30

Built for This Generation

Eden Pulse is Eden Life's vibrant youth and young adult community for those aged 13 to 30. It's a space where young people encounter God authentically, build genuine friendships, and are discipled to live purposeful, Spirit-filled lives.

From Sunday gatherings to mid-week discipleship and special youth events, Eden Pulse provides the community, mentorship, and spiritual formation that this generation needs to thrive in every area of life.

What We Do

How Eden Pulse Works

Every Sunday

Sunday Gathering

Eden Pulse gathers with the full church on Sundays for service, then connects in their own fellowship time. Young adults are actively welcomed into every area of church life, from serving to leading.

Weekly

Discipleship Groups

Small discipleship groups meet weekly for Bible study, prayer, and accountability. These intimate groups are where lasting friendships form and faith gets rooted in daily life.

Throughout the Year

Youth Events

From retreats and outreaches to creative nights and leadership development intensives, Eden Pulse events are designed to challenge, inspire, and connect young people in meaningful ways.

Your People Are Here

Join Eden Pulse

Whether you're new to faith or grew up in church, Eden Pulse has a place for you. Come as you are — your next chapter starts here.
