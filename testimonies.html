Stories of His Faithfulness

"

I was diagnosed with a condition the doctors said was irreversible, but after a night of prayer at SNOW, I returned for my check-up and the doctors were stunned — there was nothing left to treat. God healed me completely, and I have the medical reports to prove it.

Ngozi A. · Eden Life Member
"

I had lived far from God for over a decade, convinced He wanted nothing to do with me after all I had done. A colleague invited me to Eden Life, and the moment Pastor Gbenga began to preach, I felt like the message was written specifically for me. I gave my life to Christ that Sunday and have never looked back.

Emeka O. · Eden Life Member
"

My business had been struggling for three years and I was on the verge of shutting everything down. I sowed a seed of obedience at a giving Sunday and committed the business to God in prayer. Within sixty days, three new contracts came in that turned everything around — today we employ fourteen people.

Babatunde F. · Eden Life Member
"

My husband and I had been separated for two years and divorce papers were already being drawn up. A friend brought us both to a couples' session at Eden Life and God began to rebuild what we had broken. We renewed our vows here six months later, and our marriage is stronger than it has ever been.

Chidinma & Kelechi N. · Eden Life Members
"

After years of applying for roles far below my qualifications, I attended the Soar Conference and a word was spoken over people in the professional sphere. Within three weeks I received an offer for a director-level position at a multinational I had dreamed of joining for years. I know it was God's hand.

Adaeze M. · Eden Life Member
"

I relocated to Lagos alone and spent my first year deeply isolated, struggling with depression. Joining a Belong Group at Eden Life changed everything — these people became my family. The love I found here reminded me that God places the lonely in families, and I have not felt alone since.

Tunde B. · Eden Life Member
