Leadership

Servant leaders committed to equipping every person at Eden Life for a life of faith, purpose, and impact.

Senior Pastor

Pastor Gbenga Ajibola

Pastor Gbenga Ajibola is the founding and senior pastor of Eden Life Experience Centre. With a passion for the Word of God and a heart for people, he has dedicated over 15 years to building a community where every believer is equipped to live a life of exploits.

His teaching ministry is marked by clarity, depth, and practical application — bridging the truth of Scripture with the realities of everyday life. Under his leadership, Eden Life has grown from a small gathering into a multi-campus church with a global online presence.

Pastor Gbenga is committed to raising the next generation of leaders through structured discipleship, mentorship, and the Edenlife Academy programme.
