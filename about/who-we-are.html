Who We Are

A Christ-centred church in Lagos, committed to equipping every person for a life of faith, purpose, and lasting impact.

Our Story

Founded on Faith.
Built on Community.

Eden Life Experience Centre was founded by Pastor Gbenga Ajibola over 15 years ago with a simple but powerful vision: to create a community where people encounter God, discover their purpose, and go out as world-changers.

What began as a small gathering in Lagos has grown into a multi-campus church with thousands of members across the Mainland and Island, and an online community spanning continents. We remain anchored to the same values that started it all — the Word, prayer, and authentic community.

Our Mission

We Equip People for Christ and a Life of Exploits

Every programme, sermon, and community gathering at Eden Life is designed to help you grow deeper in your relationship with Christ and go further in your purpose.

A Generation Fully Alive in Christ

We envision a generation of Spirit-filled believers who are spiritually rooted, professionally excellent, and missionally engaged in every sphere of society.

Core Beliefs

What We
Believe

Our faith is rooted in the Bible and centred on the person of Jesus Christ.

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God — the complete and sufficient guide for faith and practice. Every teaching and decision at Eden Life is measured against Scripture.

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully divine and fully human. He lived a sinless life, died on the cross as our substitute, rose bodily from the dead, and is now seated at the right hand of the Father. He is the only way to God.

We believe salvation is a free gift from God, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not earned by works. Every person who confesses Jesus as Lord and believes in their heart that God raised Him from the dead will be saved (Romans 10:9).

We believe the Holy Spirit indwells every believer, empowering them to live a godly life. We embrace all the gifts of the Spirit as described in 1 Corinthians 12 and Ephesians 4, and believe they are active and necessary in the Church today.

We believe the local church is God's primary vehicle for discipleship, mission, and care. Membership in a local church is not optional — it is where we are known, challenged, and deployed for Kingdom purposes.

We believe every human being is created in the image of God (Genesis 1:27) and possesses inherent worth and dignity. This compels us to serve our community, fight injustice, and treat every person with compassion regardless of background.
