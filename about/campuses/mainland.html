The Mainland Campus is our original home — a Spirit-filled, Word-centred community in the heart of Lagos Mainland. Every service includes vibrant praise and worship, practical Bible teaching, and time for ministry and prayer.

Eden Kids runs during both services, providing an age-appropriate, safe, and engaging environment for children from nursery through pre-teen. No reservation needed — just show up.
