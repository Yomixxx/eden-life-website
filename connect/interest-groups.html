Interest Groups

More than Sunday. Eden Life's Interest Groups connect you with church members who share your life stage, profession, or passion — building community around common ground.

Connect Deeper

Find Your People

Interest Groups bring together Eden Life members who share a common life stage, vocation, or passion. Whether you're a working professional, entrepreneur, parent, or creative, there's a group built around your world.

Career

Professionals Network

For working professionals navigating career, faith, and impact. Connects corporate, public sector, and NGO workers through prayer, mentorship, and peer learning.

Join This Group
Business

Entrepreneurs Guild

A community of faith-driven business owners and entrepreneurs. Focused on integrating Kingdom values into business, collaborative growth, and practical business wisdom.

Join This Group
Marriage

Married Couples

For couples who want to grow together. Covers communication, finances, parenting, and building a Christ-centred home. A space for honest conversation in a safe community.

Join This Group
Singles

Singles & Young Professionals

For single adults navigating faith, identity, purpose, and relationships. A community that encourages wholeness, intentional growth, and thriving in every season of life.

Join This Group
Arts

Creatives & Arts

For artists, designers, musicians, writers, content creators, and anyone in the creative space. Explores faith and creativity, and how art can be an act of worship and Kingdom expression.

Join This Group
Family

Parents Community

A support and growth community for parents at every stage — from expecting parents to those with teenagers. Covers parenting with faith, discipline, communication, and raising purpose-driven children.
