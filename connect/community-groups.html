Belong Groups

Real community happens in small groups. Belong Groups are where you'll find your people, study the Word together, and do life with others who are growing in the same direction.

Small Groups

You Were Made to Belong

Belong Groups are Eden Life's small group communities — intimate gatherings of 8–15 people who meet weekly or bi-weekly for Bible study, prayer, and authentic fellowship. They are the heartbeat of community life at Eden Life.

Whether you're new to faith, new to the city, or simply looking for deeper connection beyond Sunday service, a Belong Group is the next step. Groups are available on the Mainland, on the Island, and online for members anywhere in the world.

Groups Near You

We have Belong Groups meeting across Lagos and online. Find the one that fits your location and schedule.

Mainland Groups

Groups meeting across the Lagos Mainland — including Ogudu, Gbagada, Ikeja, Ketu, Ojota, and surrounding areas. Meetings are typically held on weekday evenings or Saturday mornings.

Island Groups

Groups on the Lagos Island — serving communities in Ajah, Lekki, Victoria Island, Sangotedo, and environs. Flexible schedules to fit Island life and work rhythms.

Join an Island Group

Online Groups

Live anywhere in the world and still belong. Our online Belong Groups meet via video call and are a full community experience — not just a watch party. Open to all Eden Life online members.
