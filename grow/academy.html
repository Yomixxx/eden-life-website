Edenlife Academy

Level 2 of the Eden Life Discipleship Pathway. A deep-dive into biblical knowledge, theological understanding, and Christ-centred living.

Level 2 — Biblical Formation

Go Deeper in the Word

Edenlife Academy is the second level of Eden Life's discipleship pathway, designed for believers who have completed Growth Steps and are ready for a richer, more structured engagement with the Word of God.

The Academy curriculum equips students with the tools to interpret Scripture accurately, understand core Christian doctrines, and integrate their faith into every dimension of life. It is a formative experience for those who want to serve, lead, or simply know God more deeply.

Apply for Academy
Curriculum

What You'll Study

Module 1

Biblical Hermeneutics

Learn the principles and methods for interpreting Scripture correctly. Understand context, genre, language, and application — so you can rightly handle the Word of truth in your personal study and teaching.

Module 2

Systematic Theology

A structured study of the major doctrines of the Christian faith — including theology proper, Christology, pneumatology, soteriology, ecclesiology, and eschatology — grounded in Scripture and applied to life.

Module 3

Christian Living

Practical instruction on walking out the Christian life — covering spiritual disciplines, ethics, relationships, vocation, stewardship, and how to live as a Kingdom citizen in every sphere of society.
