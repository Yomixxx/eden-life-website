Growth Steps

Level 1 of the Eden Life Discipleship Pathway. A free, foundational class for every believer who wants to build their faith on solid ground.

Level 1 — Foundations

Start Your Journey Here

Growth Steps is Eden Life's foundational discipleship class — a free, structured, and highly practical introduction to the core truths of the Christian faith. It is designed for new believers, returning Christians, and anyone who wants to build their faith on a sure foundation.

Growth Steps is free to all Eden Life members and can be taken in-person or online. Classes run in cohorts throughout the year. Completing Growth Steps is the prerequisite for advancing to Edenlife Academy (Level 2).

What You Will Learn

  • Salvation — Understanding what it means to be born again and secure in Christ
  • The Bible — How to read, study, and apply Scripture in your daily life
  • Prayer — Building a consistent and effective prayer life
  • The Holy Spirit — Who the Holy Spirit is, how He works, and how to be filled
  • The Church — Why church membership and community are essential to growth
  • Stewardship — Honouring God with your time, talents, and finances

The Discipleship Pathway

Growth Steps is Level 1 of Eden Life's four-level discipleship pathway. Complete it and advance to:

  • Level 2 — Edenlife Academy
  • Level 3 — Basic Leadership Training
  • Level 4 — Advanced Leadership Training
