Activate 2027

A landmark Eden Life conference — a generational gathering to activate the church for the next era of Kingdom advancement, spiritual awakening, and transformative impact.

Landmark Conference

Activated for the
Next Era

Activate 2027 is one of the most significant events in Eden Life's history — a multi-day landmark conference designed to mark a new era of spiritual momentum, Kingdom alignment, and church-wide activation. It is a gathering for the entire Eden Life family and beyond.

Activate 2027 will bring together ministers, leaders, young people, professionals, and believers from across Lagos and the global Eden Life community for a concentrated season of teaching, impartation, worship, and commissioning. Those who attend will leave with fresh fire, clear direction, and divine empowerment.

Activate 2027 Themes

  • Awakening — A fresh encounter with the Holy Spirit that reignites passion, hunger, and spiritual vitality
  • Identity & Destiny — Reclaiming your Kingdom identity and stepping into the fullness of your God-given assignment
  • Generational Shift — Raising and releasing the next generation of leaders who will carry the church forward
  • Mission & Dominion — The church fulfilling its mandate to disciple nations and transform every sphere of society
  • Commissioning — A sacred time of corporate commissioning, sending, and consecration for the era ahead
