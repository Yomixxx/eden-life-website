Soar Conference

Eden Life's annual leadership conference — equipping believers to rise higher in purpose, influence, and Kingdom impact across every sphere of society.

Annual Conference

Equipped to Lead. Called to Rise.

Soar Conference is Eden Life's flagship annual leadership event. Bringing together leaders, professionals, ministers, and Kingdom-minded believers for days of intensive teaching, impartation, and strategic equipping.

The conference draws some of the finest voices in Christian leadership and ministry, providing attendees with fresh vision, practical tools, and powerful encounters that translate into real-world transformation. Soar is not just an event — it is an upgrade.

Soar Conference Includes

  • Keynote Sessions — High-impact teaching from Pastor Gbenga and invited guest speakers
  • Leadership Workshops — Practical breakout sessions on ministry, business, and personal leadership
  • Worship & Impartation — Powerful nights of worship and personal ministry for breakthrough and fresh vision
  • Networking — Curated connection opportunities with other leaders and professionals
  • Live Broadcast — Streamed online for remote participants and a global audience
