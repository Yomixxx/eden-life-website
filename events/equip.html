EQUIP

Discipleship Training — Eden Life's intensive programme immersing participants in Scripture, spiritual formation, and hands-on ministry practice.

Intensive Programme

Trained. Sent. Multiplied.

EQUIP is Eden Life's intensive discipleship training programme, designed to rapidly and thoroughly equip believers for active Kingdom service. More immersive than a regular class, EQUIP combines deep doctrinal teaching with practical ministry application.

Whether you're preparing for full-time ministry, stepping into a leadership role, or simply hungry to go deeper in your faith and effectiveness, EQUIP provides the foundation, tools, and community to get you there.

EQUIP Curriculum

  • Scripture & Doctrine — In-depth Bible study and core Christian doctrine for confident, grounded faith
  • Spiritual Disciplines — Prayer, fasting, Word meditation, and building a sustainable devotional life
  • Evangelism & Witness — Practical training in sharing the gospel effectively in everyday contexts
  • Ministry Practice — Supervised hands-on ministry experience in real church and community settings
  • Identity & Calling — Discovering and aligning with your God-given purpose for maximum Kingdom impact
