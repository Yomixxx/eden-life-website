SNOW

Supernatural Night of Wonders — Eden Life's annual extended worship and prayer night where heaven meets earth in extraordinary ways.

Annual Event

Where Worship Meets the Supernatural

SNOW — Supernatural Night of Wonders — is Eden Life's annual extended worship and prayer night. It is one of the most anticipated events in the Eden Life calendar: a night where the church gathers for hours of unbroken praise, deep intercession, and encounters with the living God.

SNOW is not a concert or performance — it is a sacred gathering where the presence of God is both invited and expected. Healings, breakthroughs, salvations, and fresh encounters with the Holy Spirit are characteristic of this night. First-timers and long-time members come away transformed.

What to Expect at SNOW

  • Extended Worship — Hours of live, Spirit-led worship from the Eden Life worship team
  • Corporate Intercession — Powerful, united prayer for individuals, the church, and the nation
  • Healing & Ministry — Ministry time for healing, deliverance, and personal breakthrough
  • Prophetic Moments — Spontaneous prophetic words and decrees released over the congregation
  • Live Broadcast — Streamed live on YouTube for those who cannot attend in person
