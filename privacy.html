Privacy
Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information at Eden Life Experience Centre.

Last updated: May 2025

1

Who We Are

Eden Life Experience Centre ("ELEC", "we", "us", "our") is a Christ-centred church based in Lagos, Nigeria.

Website: www.edenlifeng.org  ·  Email: info@edenlifeng.org  ·  Tel: +234 909 720 9484

2

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a contact or prayer request form (name, email, phone number, message)
  • Register for membership, Growth Steps, or any ELEC programme
  • Make a donation or tithe payment online
  • Subscribe to newsletters or download resources (e-Books, study guides)
  • Plan a visit or request a free ride to church

We also automatically collect technical data (IP address, browser type, device, pages viewed, time on site) through cookies and analytics tools when you visit our website.

3

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your enquiries, prayer requests, and pastoral needs
  • Process your giving, tithes, and donations securely
  • Register you for membership classes, Growth Steps, or events
  • Send church news, sermon updates, and event announcements (with your consent)
  • Improve the performance and user experience of our website
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns (Google Ads)
4

Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function correctly. Cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

Help us understand how visitors use our site — pages visited, time on site, traffic source. Data is aggregated and anonymous.

Marketing Cookies (Google Ads)

Allow Google to measure the performance of our church advertising campaigns and serve relevant ads. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.

You may manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

5

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics & Google Ads — website analytics and advertising measurement
  • Paystack — secure online payment processing for giving and donations
  • YouTube — embedded sermon videos and live stream
  • WordPress / Web hosting — website content management and hosting infrastructure
6

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data may only be shared with trusted service providers who help us operate our website and ministry (e.g., Paystack for payment processing), or where required by Nigerian law or a court order.

7

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable laws. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time (every email includes an unsubscribe link)
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@edenlifeng.org.

9

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

10

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Online payments are processed exclusively through Paystack's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure — we never store card details on our servers.

11

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
